Hello, my name is Mika Villavert and I am a current second year student at UCR majoring in Political Science with a concentration in Public Service.

Although I am only a second year student, I have a lot of experience in jobs related to my major. In high school, I was President of Key Club where I earned multiple volunteer hours each week. Key Club greatly developed my interest in the Public Service field because it taught me more about nonprofit work and the different ways I can serve my community. Because of Key Club, I later joined Circle K at UCR where I have helped out in places like blood drives and children hospitals.

In addition to Circle K, I am currently a member of ASUCR's Civic Engagement Committee, where I promote civic engagement amongst UCR students. In this committee, I have helped draft letters for non-instructional day, which encourages the Chancellor to cancel school for students every year on Election Day.
